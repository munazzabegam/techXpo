Rules and Regulations

Eligibility:
  • Participants must have a valid mobile device that supports the game.
  • Each participant must be registered in advance to secure a spot in the competition.
  • Each participant must have the latest version of the game installed.
  • Each participant must have a stable internet connection.
Format:
  • Duo Format: Each team consists of two players.
  • Both players should be from the same college.
  • The number of matches each team will play will be specified during the competition.
Scoring System:
  • Teams will earn points based on both kills and placement in each match.
  • In case of a tie in points, tiebreakers will be determined by total damage dealt, survival time, etc.
Fair Play and Code of Conduct:
  • Emphasis on Fair Play: The integrity of the competition relies on fair play and sportsmanship. All participants are expected to uphold the spirit of fair competition throughout the event.
  • Cheating, Hacking, and Exploits: Cheating, hacking, or exploiting glitches in the game is strictly prohibited. Participants found engaging in such activities will face severe consequences, including immediate disqualification from the competition.
Consequences for Rule Violations:
  • Any violation of the rules will be thoroughly investigated, and appropriate action will be taken.
  • Consequences for rule violations may include, but are not limited to:
    1. Verbal warning for minor infractions.
    2. Round disqualification for serious rule violations.
    3. Disqualification from the entire competition for repeated or severe offenses.
Zero Tolerance Policy:
  • The event has a zero-tolerance policy for cheating and unfair practices. Participants are expected to compete with honesty and integrity. Any attempt to compromise the fairness of the competition will be dealt with swiftly and decisively.
Restart Protocol:
  • In the event of a game malfunction or critical issue affecting one or more participants, the match may be restarted at the discretion of the organizers.
Disconnects:
  • In the case of a player disconnecting during a match, the organizers will decide whether to continue the match, restart it, or take other appropriate action.
Judges Decision are Final
